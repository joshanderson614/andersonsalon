Where It All Began

A Vision Born
in Los Angeles

Anderson Beauty was founded in 2000 with a singular vision: to create a beauty sanctuary in the heart of Los Angeles where every client is treated with the artistry, care, and respect they deserve.

What began as a boutique studio has grown into one of LA's most celebrated luxury salons — a destination where top industry talent gathers and where clients return year after year because they simply won't trust anyone else.

Over a quarter century, we've built relationships that go far beyond the salon chair. We know your story, your style, and your vision. We've been there for weddings, graduations, first days, and every ordinary Tuesday in between — because great beauty isn't just for special occasions.

What Drives Us

Our Philosophy

Three principles that guide everything we do — from the products we choose to the way we greet every client at the door.

Conscious Luxury

We curate only the finest professional-grade products — sustainably sourced, rigorously tested, and chosen for their performance above all else. Luxury with a conscience.

Artistry First

Every stylist, nail tech, and esthetician at Anderson Beauty is selected not just for their skill, but for their artistic sensibility. We treat beauty as a craft — and craft as an art.

Client Partnership

We don't just execute services — we build relationships. Your goals, your lifestyle, and your unique beauty drive every decision we make on your behalf.

The Talent Behind the Brand

Meet Our Specialists

Each member of our team brings a unique depth of expertise, a passion for their craft, and a commitment to your complete satisfaction.

S
Master Stylist

Sofia Martinez

With over 18 years behind the chair, Sofia is the creative heart of Anderson Beauty. Originally trained in Madrid and later apprenticed under a Vidal Sassoon master, she brings European precision to every cut and style.

Sofia specializes in transformative haircuts, precision geometric cuts, and textured styling for all hair types. Her clientele includes actresses, musicians, and LA's most discerning style icons.

Precision Cuts Texture & Volume Bridal Styling
Color Specialist

Isabella Chen

Isabella is considered one of LA's foremost colorists, celebrated for her mastery of balayage, multi-dimensional color, and corrective work. She trained with the Wella International Color Award team in Paris.

Her philosophy: color should look like the light gave it to you. From sun-kissed naturals to bold editorial statements, Isabella creates color that moves, breathes, and evolves beautifully.

Nail Artist

Ava Williams

Ava has turned nail care into a genuine art form. Her nail art has been featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and on the runways of LA Fashion Week. She merges fine art training with technical nail mastery.

From minimalist elegance to intricate designs, Ava approaches every set with a painter's eye and a technician's precision. Her gel work is renowned for its durability and flawless finish.

Lead Esthetician

Emma Thompson

Emma is a licensed esthetician with dual certification in medical aesthetics and holistic skincare. With 14 years of experience, she has developed a deeply personalized approach to skin wellness that produces visible, lasting results.

Her skin assessments go beyond the surface — she looks at lifestyle, diet, and stress factors to create genuinely effective treatment plans. Emma's clients regularly report transformative skin improvements within just a few sessions.
